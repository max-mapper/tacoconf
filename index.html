A conference based on bicycles and tacos

Day 1: Oakland, California
Day 2: San Francisco, California
Free to attend, see map below for details

Are you serious?

Yes. We have arranged for all kinds of talks about food, culture and technology. In addition, attendees will ride their bicycles on a mini-tour of some of the best antojitos that the Bay Area offers.

What to bring

A bicycle + some cash to buy tacos. Bonus points if you bring your significant other and/or kids. TacoConf is fun for the whole family!

Be prepared to bicycle between taco trucks and taquerias and hear presentations from interesting people!

Speakers (semi-complete list): @aq @izs @burritojustice @abright @philipn @brian_mount @st_luke @digitalctzn @cfarivar @cramforce @CATacoTrucks

Each speaker will on present on a single day so come on out for both days to see them all!

Scrolling Sprites

The next step in your parallax scrolling adventure is to create some sprites!

I have cut out one of the photos in this background and use it as a sprite. It scrolls at a different speed to the background and the window, and it's this effect that's used so well on Nike Better World and other parallax sites..

Using too many sprites can slow down older computers, and you need to use some clever maths (and trial & error!) to work out their placement on the page.
